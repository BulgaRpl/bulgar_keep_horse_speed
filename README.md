This script removes the automatic slowdown that normally occurs when your horse approaches another horse or a wagon. It also disables the mechanic that allows jumping from your horse onto another horse or wagon. As a result, your riding speed remains consistent and unaffected by nearby entities, ensuring smoother and uninterrupted movement at all times.
